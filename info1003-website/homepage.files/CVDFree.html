Did you know?

One in every 6 Australian is diagnosed with CVD disease. Cardiovascular disease (CVD) is the leading cause of death and disease burden in Australia. The number of people living with cardiovascular disease is increasing due to factors including population ageing and imporved treatments. Sadly, most people are not aware of the CVD disease.

We are here to help.

Our purpose is to increase the awareness of cardiovascular disease (CVD) in Australia and to educate people on how to avoid CVD.

Join the movement today #CVDfree.
