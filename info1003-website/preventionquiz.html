PREVENTION QUIZ

b) Eating more packaged food especially those high in sodium and sugar
c) Consuming healthier food like fish, sardines, variety of fruits and lean meat or poultry
d) Don’t do any physical activity

2. Is drinking alcohol increases the chance of getting CVD?

b) No, consuming any amount of alcohol will not increase the chance of developing CVD
c) Moderate or small amount alcohol would reduce the risk of developing CVD
d) Large amount of alcohol helps to reduce the risk of developing CVD

3. Which of the following factors is more important to reduce the risk of developing CVD?

b) Exercise vigorously every day for at least 30 minutes
c) Having a healthy diet is the utmost important factor to reduce the possibility of CVD
d) All of the factors above is important to reduce the risk of developing CVD

d) Fish, shellfish and poultry
