About Us

What is CVDFree?

CVD Free is a non-government organization that aims to educate everyone about the perils of CVD and raise awareness of its causes and effects. Our team is currently operating out of Sydney, Australia, but plan to branch internationally in the future with help of our sponsors and other donations!
The organization was founded in early 2015 by a team of four talented and brilliant researchers. The team consists of magnificent web designers, sensational research analysts and excellent health specialists.
With your help, we can be CVD FREE!
